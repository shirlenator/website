1/15/2013 I wish I understood fluids... Sigh.

If you watch smoke streaming from a stick of incense, backlit by a candle, you will feel calm. What makes the stream unravel into such twirls, then curls and wisps? What obstacles, what pleasures await the smoke in the air invisible to me? I am powerless -- there is no pause button. Here, in the breezelessness of my living room: turbulence I will never know.


1/9/2013 Candlelight

The two of us consumed by tasks
we set ourselves to do.
Grateful for my companion's time
though wary
that her patience and her
light consume her.
