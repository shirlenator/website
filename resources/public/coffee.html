Shilei (Shirley) Zheng


Two weekends ago I went to SC with my mom and picked up some fancy [beans]. My weapon of choice was my new hario v60 dripper. The coffee came out without aroma, and adjusting the water volume and temperature only affected the acidity. I think the only thing I couldn't mask was the biting acidity. Like hot stomach acid.

The folks down at my favorite coffee shop were serving the Karie by chance. According to them, I was under-extracting, and I could try using hotter water and pouring more slowly. I gave this a try, and the results were astonishing! Tart berries.

Faith in curiosity, love of this world restored.
